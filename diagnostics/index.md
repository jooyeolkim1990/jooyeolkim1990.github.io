# Diagnostic Work

<p class="index-lede">Diagnostic work turns vague discomfort into a named problem, reviewable dimensions, responsibility and appeal paths, and a decision-ready output. Public samples reveal the diagnostic surface while keeping private engines and thresholds bounded.</p>

## Typical public output

A public diagnostic page normally shows:

1. the situation or claim being reviewed;
2. the named structural problem;
3. the review dimensions;
4. the responsibility, escalation, appeal, or rollback path;
5. the decision-facing conclusion;
6. the boundary between the public sample and the private analytical engine.
